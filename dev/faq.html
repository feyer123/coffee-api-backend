Frequently Asked Questions

At Essential Service Coffee, every bag starts with carefully selected single-origin green beans - no blends, just one source per roast so you can taste the true character of each origin. We source from multiple trusted suppliers to offer a rotating variety of standout coffees throughout the year.

Unlike large roasters, we hand-roast in small batches with precision and care, not automation. Each roast is crafted to highlight the bean’s natural flavor—not masked by dark roasts or blending. We roast to order, and the roast date is stamped right on the label, so you know exactly how fresh your coffee is when it arrives.Our goal isn’t volume—it’s quality, character, and connection.

We roast to order to ensure you get the freshest coffee possible. Most experts agree coffee hits its peak flavor between 4 and 14 days after roasting, so we identify the roast date on your bag label. Once you place your order, we’ll confirm the next available roast date and ship it shortly after, so your beans arrive tasting their best.
