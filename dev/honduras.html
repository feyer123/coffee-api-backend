About This Roast

Honduras Del Campo HG EP is an everyday, drinkable coffee with natural caramel, citrus, chocolate flavors combining with a clean finish. "Del Campo" translates to "from the countryside". The beans are sourced from various smallholder farmers within the western highlands of Honduras rather than a specific single estate. "HG" stands for "High Grown", indicating that these coffee beans were cultivated at altitudes between 1,200 and 1,350 meters above sea level. This higher elevation often contributes to a denser bean, which can lead to a more complex and flavorful cup. "EP" or European Preparation signifies that the green coffee beans have been meticulously hand-sorted to remove any defects and foreign materials, ensuring a higher quality standard. These beans are washed processed. This method involves removing the fruit pulp before drying the beans, resulting in a cleaner and brighter flavor profile compared to natural or dry-processed coffees.
